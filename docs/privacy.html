Overview

Positive Phill is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device with no data collection, no user accounts, and no cloud synchronization.

Privacy-First Guarantee: Everything stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information.

Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Custom affirmations
  • Mood tags and tracking data
  • Streak information
  • Reminder preferences

No User Accounts

Positive Phill does not require user accounts, registration, or any personal information. You can use the app completely anonymously.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information or usage data. Everything stays on your device.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Local-Only Operation

  • All preferences are stored locally using device storage
  • Reminders are managed by your device's local notification system
  • Text-to-Speech uses your device's built-in TTS engine
  • No internet connection is required for core functionality

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. We recommend using your device's security features (screen lock, encryption) to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our documentation website.
