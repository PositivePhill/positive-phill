What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include:

  1. Device Information: Type of device and operating system version
  2. App Version: The version of Positive Phill you're using
  3. Issue Description: A clear description of the problem or question
  4. Steps to Reproduce: If applicable, steps to reproduce the issue

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 2-3 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Do I need an account?

No. Positive Phill works completely without user accounts or registration.

Does the app require internet?

No. The app works entirely offline after installation.

How do I backup my data?

Your data is stored locally on your device. Please use your device's built-in backup features to protect your data.
