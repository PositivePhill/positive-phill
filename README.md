# Positive Phill

A privacy-first positive affirmations app designed to help you maintain a positive mindset through personalized affirmations and daily encouragement.

## Features

- **Privacy-First Design**: All data stored locally on your device - no accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking
- **Mood Tags**: Categorize and track your emotional journey with customizable mood tags
- **Streak Tracking**: Build healthy habits with daily streak tracking and reminders
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Listen to your affirmations with natural voice synthesis
- **Local Reminders**: Set personalized reminders that work entirely on your device
- **No Internet Required**: Works completely offline after installation

## Privacy

Positive Phill is built with privacy as a core principle. All your data stays on your device. See [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md) for details.
