# Support

Thank you for using Positive Phill! We're here to help with any questions or issues you may have.

## Contact Information

For support inquiries, please email us at:

**support@possummattern.com**

## What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include:

1. **Device Information**: Type of device and operating system version
2. **App Version**: The version of Positive Phill you're using
3. **Issue Description**: A clear description of the problem or question
4. **Steps to Reproduce**: If applicable, steps to reproduce the issue

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 2-3 business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my data private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for more details.

### Do I need an account?

No. Positive Phill works completely without user accounts or registration.

### Does the app require internet?

No. The app works entirely offline after installation.

### How do I backup my data?

Your data is stored locally on your device. Please use your device's built-in backup features to protect your data.

## Emergency Resources

**Important**: If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact:

- **Emergency Services**: 911 (US) or your local emergency number
- **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline**: 988 (US)
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741 (US)

Positive Phill is not a substitute for professional mental health care.
